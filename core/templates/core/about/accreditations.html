State & National Approval

Fully Licensed & State Approved

When you train at Glory Nursing, you train at an institution recognized by the state of Oklahoma and national certification bodies.

Nurse Aide Registry

Oklahoma State Department of Health – Nurse Aide Registry (OSDH-NAR)

Our CNA program is approved by OSDH-NAR, which maintains the Oklahoma Nurse Aide Registry. Graduates are eligible for registry listing upon exam passage.

BLS/CPR

American Heart Association (AHA)

Our BLS and CPR courses are taught by AHA-certified instructors and meet American Heart Association standards for healthcare providers.

Why Accreditation Matters

Attending an accredited school means your certification is recognized by employers, your training meets professional standards, and you are eligible to sit for state and national exams. At Glory Nursing, every program you enroll in meets or exceeds the requirements set by Oklahoma state authorities and national certification bodies.
