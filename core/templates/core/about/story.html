Founded With Purpose

Built by Healthcare Professionals, For Future Heroes

Glory Nursing Healthcare Training School was co-founded by Charles and Binui — a husband-and-wife team with deep roots in healthcare and a burning passion for giving back to their community.

The school was born out of a simple but powerful belief: that quality healthcare training should be accessible, affordable, and personal. Too many aspiring healthcare workers were falling through the cracks of large institutions. Charles and Binui set out to change that.

Today, Glory Nursing is a state-approved vocational institution proudly serving Oklahoma City and beyond — training the next generation of CNAs, CMAs, Phlebotomy Technicians, and allied health professionals.

Meet the Founders

The People Behind Glory Nursing

Charles

Co-Founder & Director

Originally from Ghana, Charles worked in healthcare in Africa before earning his nursing degree in the United States in 2015. He brings years of hands-on experience across home care, senior centers, and hospital settings.

His deep commitment to patient care and professional excellence drives the quality standards at Glory Nursing every single day.

Binui

Co-Founder & Lead Instructor

Originally from Cameroon, Binui moved to Oklahoma in 2011 and began her healthcare journey as a Certified Nursing Assistant. Through hard work and dedication, she earned her Registered Nurse (RN) degree.

Her own journey from CNA to RN gives her a unique perspective that inspires and motivates every student at Glory Nursing.

Our Journey

Glory Nursing Through the Years

2011

Binui Arrives in Oklahoma

Binui moves to Oklahoma City and begins her healthcare career as a CNA, discovering a passion for patient care and teaching others.

2015

Charles Earns Nursing Degree

Charles completes his nursing degree in the US after years of healthcare work in Ghana, bringing international experience to the team.

2018

Glory Nursing is Founded

Charles and Binui open Glory Nursing Healthcare Training School in Oklahoma City with a vision to make quality healthcare training accessible to all.

2020

State Approval & Expansion

Glory Nursing receives full state approval from OBPVS and OBN, and expands its program offerings to include CMA, Phlebotomy, EKG, and more.

2024

500+ Graduates & Growing

Over 500 students have graduated from Glory Nursing and gone on to careers in hospitals, nursing homes, home health agencies, and clinics across Oklahoma.

Today

Brighter Futures Begin Here

Glory Nursing continues to grow, serving more students, adding programs, and fulfilling its mission of empowering healthcare heroes one graduate at a time.
