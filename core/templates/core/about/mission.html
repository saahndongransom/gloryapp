Our Mission

Why We Exist

"To provide high-quality, accessible, and affordable healthcare training that empowers every student to build a successful career and make a difference in the lives of patients and communities."

Our Motto: Brighter Futures Begin Here.

Our Vision

Where We're Headed

To become Oklahoma's most trusted and student-centered healthcare training institution — known not just for our certifications, but for the lives we transform and the communities we strengthen.

  • Expand program offerings every year
  • Serve more underrepresented communities
  • Build the largest CNA alumni network in Oklahoma
  • Partner with hospitals and clinics statewide
Core Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Every decision we make, every class we teach, and every student we serve is guided by these core values.

01

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in education, training, and student outcomes. Good enough is never good enough at Glory Nursing.

02

Integrity

We operate with complete honesty and transparency in everything we do — from admissions to graduation. You can always trust us.

03

Compassion

We genuinely care about every student, every patient, and every community we serve. Healthcare starts with compassion — and so do we.

04

Innovation

We constantly update our curriculum and teaching methods to stay ahead of healthcare industry demands and give students a competitive edge.

05

Community

We believe in the power of community — supporting each other, lifting each other up, and giving back to the neighborhoods that raised us.

06

Accessibility

Quality healthcare education should be available to everyone. We work hard to keep tuition affordable and offer flexible scheduling for all lifestyles.
