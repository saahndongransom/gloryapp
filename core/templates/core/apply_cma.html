Required Documents

Your application packet includes all required forms:

  • CMA Attestation (OSDH Form)
  • Enrollment Application
  • Affidavit of Lawful Presence
  • National ID Card
  • Social security Card
  • Clinical Tasks Policy
  • Photo/Media Release Waiver
  • Grievance & Refund Policy
  • Background Check Waiver

⚠️ CMA Prerequisites

  • Age 16 or older
  • High School Diploma or GED
  • Active Oklahoma CNA certification
  • Minimum 6 months CNA experience
  • No abuse notations on CNA record
