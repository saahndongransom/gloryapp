Our Story

Founded by Healthcare Professionals, For Future Heroes

Glory Nursing Healthcare Training School was co-founded by Charles and Binui, a husband-and-wife team with deep roots in healthcare and a passion for giving back.

Charles, originally from Ghana, worked in healthcare in Africa before earning his nursing degree in the United States in 2015. He brings years of experience in home care, senior centers, and hospitals.

Binui, originally from Cameroon, moved to Oklahoma in 2011. She began her healthcare career as a CNA and worked her way up to become a Registered Nurse (RN). Together, they founded Glory Nursing to train the next generation of compassionate healthcare professionals.
