Welcome to Glory Nursing

Your Path to a Rewarding Healthcare Career

Glory Nursing Healthcare Training School in Oklahoma City is a state-approved vocational institution dedicated to empowering students with the knowledge, skills, and certifications needed to thrive in healthcare.

Founded by Charles and Binui — both experienced healthcare professionals originally from Ghana and Cameroon — Glory Nursing brings a personal, mission-driven approach to every program we offer. You're not just a student here; you're family.

  • Programs completable in as few as 2 weeks
  • Flexible: weekday, evening, weekend & online options
  • Expert, licensed & certified instructors
  • State-of-the-art simulation labs
  • Clinical placements through local partnerships
