Funding Your Education

Financial Assistance Options

Glory Nursing partners with multiple organizations and programs to help you cover tuition costs. Many students attend at little to no out-of-pocket expense.

Oklahoma Workforce (OKDHS / OJA)

Sponsored Funding

The Oklahoma Office of Workforce Development and Oklahoma Employment Security Commission provide funding for eligible residents to attend approved vocational training programs — including all Glory Nursing programs.

  • May cover full tuition for qualifying students
  • Available to unemployed or underemployed Oklahomans
  • Our admissions team can help you apply
Ask If You Qualify

Native American / Indian Tribe Programs

Tribal Sponsorship

Many Oklahoma tribal nations offer educational sponsorship and funding programs for enrolled tribal members pursuing vocational training. Glory Nursing is an approved training provider for several tribal programs.

  • Available to enrolled tribal members in Oklahoma
  • May cover tuition in full
  • Contact your tribal education office or ask us to help connect you
Ask About Tribal Funding

Employer Sponsorships

Employer-Funded

Many healthcare employers — hospitals, nursing homes, home health agencies, and clinics — will sponsor employees who want to earn additional certifications. We invoice employers directly and handle the paperwork.

  • Employer pays tuition directly to Glory Nursing
  • We can send a formal proposal to your employer
  • Works with hospitals, LTCs, home health agencies
Request Employer Letter

Flexible Payment Plans

Self-Pay Option

If you're paying out-of-pocket, we offer flexible installment options so you can start your program without paying everything upfront. Plans vary by program length and tuition amount.

  • Split payment: 50% at enrollment, 50% at start
  • Monthly installments for longer programs
  • No interest on in-house payment plans
Discuss Payment Plans

Student Referral Bonus

Refer & Save

Refer a friend, family member, or colleague to Glory Nursing and receive a discount or bonus credit toward your own tuition or future courses. Investing in someone else's future pays back.

  • Earn credit when your referral enrolls
  • Applicable toward current or future programs
  • Ask admissions for current referral bonus amount
