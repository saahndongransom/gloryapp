Step by Step

How to Apply to Glory Nursing

Our enrollment process is simple and straightforward. Most students are contacted within 1–2 business days of applying.

1

Choose Your Program

Browse our 8+ state-approved healthcare training programs and select the one that fits your career goals and schedule preferences. Not sure? Contact admissions — we're happy to help you decide.

2

Submit Your Application

Complete our short online application form. It takes less than 5 minutes. You'll be asked for basic personal information and your program of interest.

3

Admissions Review

Our admissions team reviews your application and reaches out within 1–2 business days to discuss your enrollment options, schedule, and any questions you may have.

4

Submit Required Documents

Provide a government-issued ID, high school diploma or GED, immunization records, Social Security card, and any other program-specific documents. Our team will guide you through this.

5

Enrollment & Payment

Complete your enrollment paperwork and arrange tuition payment. We offer payment plans and work with Oklahoma Workforce, Indian Tribe programs, and employer sponsorships.

6

Attend Orientation & Start!

Attend your program orientation, receive your materials, and begin your journey toward a rewarding healthcare career. Welcome to the Glory Nursing family!

Be Prepared

Document Checklist

Gather these documents before or during your admissions process to speed up enrollment.

Identity Documents

  • Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, state ID, or passport)
  • Social Security card or work authorization document
  • Birth certificate (if applicable)

Education Records

  • High school diploma or GED certificate
  • Official transcripts (if available)
  • Prior healthcare certifications (if applicable)

Health Records

  • Immunization records (MMR, Hepatitis B, Varicella, TB)
  • TB test results (must be within 12 months)
  • Physical exam clearance (for clinical programs)

Background & Clearance

  • Background check authorization (we'll guide you)
  • Drug screening consent form
  • CPR certification (if already held)

Not sure if you have everything? Our admissions team will help you gather what you need.
