General Admissions Requirements

The following requirements apply to most Glory Nursing programs. Program-specific requirements are listed below.

General Requirements (All Programs)

Age: 16 or older

All applicants must be at least 16 years of age at the time of enrollment.

High School Diploma or GED

A copy of your diploma or GED certificate is required at enrollment. Foreign diplomas may require a translation.

Government-Issued Photo ID

A valid driver's license, state ID, or passport is required. ID must be current and not expired.

Social Security Card or Work Authorization

Required for enrollment and state exam registration. Non-citizens must provide valid work authorization documents.

Health & Clinical Requirements

Immunization Records

Required for all clinical programs: MMR (2 doses), Varicella (2 doses or titer), Hepatitis B series, and annual influenza vaccine. TB test results must be within the past 12 months.

Physical Exam Clearance

Required for CNA, CMA, HHA, Phlebotomy, EKG, and CCMA programs. A physician must verify you are physically capable of performing clinical duties.

Background Check

A criminal background check is required before clinical placement. Certain criminal histories may affect eligibility for state certification exams. Our team can advise you on this before enrollment.

Drug Screening

Required for clinical externship placement. Partners may require a 10-panel drug screen prior to placement.

Program-Specific Requirements

🏥 CNA

No prior experience required. General requirements only.

💊 CMA

Must hold a current, active Oklahoma CNA certification before enrolling.

🏠 HHA

No prior experience required. General requirements only.

❤️ BLS / CPR

No prerequisites. Open to healthcare workers and general public. Renewal and initial options available.

🩸 Phlebotomy

No prior experience required. Must complete physical exam clearance before externship.

📈 EKG Technician

No prior experience required. Basic science knowledge helpful but not required.

🩺 CCMA

No prior experience required. Full health clearance required before externship.

💻 Billing & Coding

Basic computer literacy required. No clinical health clearance needed (no clinical hours).
