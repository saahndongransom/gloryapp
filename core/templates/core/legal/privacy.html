Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you apply for a program or create an account on our Learning Management System (LMS), we collect:

  • Personal identification (name, date of birth, Social Security Number)
  • Contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Education and employment history
  • Payment information (processed securely via Square; we do not store card numbers)
  • Course progress, quiz scores, and certificates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process enrollment applications and payments
  • Provide access to course materials and track your progress
  • Issue certificates upon course completion
  • Communicate with you about your enrollment, classes, and account
  • Comply with Oklahoma State Department of Health requirements for nurse aide certification

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • The Oklahoma State Department of Health / Nurse Aide Registry for certification purposes
  • Square, Inc. for payment processing
  • Third-party background check providers (only with your consent)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure password storage, and access-controlled databases. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain student records as required by Oklahoma state regulations, generally for a minimum of one year after program completion, and longer where required by law.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at glorynursing@yahoo.com. Note that certain records must be retained for regulatory compliance.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for session management and to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our programs are intended for individuals 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
